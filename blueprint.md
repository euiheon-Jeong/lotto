# Lotto Number Generator & Analysis Hub (30-Page Blueprint)

## Overview
A comprehensive, content-rich web platform for lotto enthusiasts. Transformed from a single-page tool into a multi-page authority site to meet Google AdSense "High Quality" standards.

## Current State & Features
- Lotto generation tool (React-style logic in Vanilla JS).
- Dark/Light mode toggle.
- Affiliate inquiry form & Disqus comments.
- **Completed:** 29 unique, high-quality content pages across 4 directories.
- Full internal linking and consistent navigation.

## Site Architecture (29 Pages)
### 1. Core Tools (2 Pages)
- `tools/random-picker.html`: Simple random number generator.
- `tools/pattern-checker.html`: Tool to check if numbers follow common patterns.

### 2. Strategy & Analysis (10 Pages) - `/strategy/`
- `probability-math.html`: The science behind lotto odds.
- `hot-cold-numbers.html`: Analyzing frequency of past winning numbers.
- `wheeling-systems.html`: Explanation of lottery wheeling systems.
- `quick-pick-vs-manual.html`: Pros and cons of automated selection.
- `budget-management.html`: Playing responsibly and setting limits.
- `number-syndicates.html`: How group play increases odds.
- `historical-trends.html`: Patterns found in 10 years of lotto data.
- `unlucky-numbers.html`: Numbers people avoid and why.
- `statistical-anomalies.html`: Rare occurrences in lottery history.
- `selection-algorithms.html`: How our generator's "Smart Pick" works.

### 3. Culture & Psychology (10 Pages) - `/culture/`
- `psychology-of-luck.html`: Why we believe in lucky charms.
- `dream-interpretations.html`: Classic "lotto dreams" and their numbers.
- `winner-stories.html`: Inspiring stories of regular people winning big.
- `winning-mistakes.html`: What NOT to do after winning the jackpot.
- `global-lotto-traditions.html`: How different cultures play the lottery.
- `powerball-vs-mega-millions.html`: Comparing the world's biggest games.
- `celebrity-winners.html`: Famous people who won (or lost) on lotto.
- `lotto-in-movies.html`: Memorable lottery scenes in cinema.
- `emotional-rollercoaster.html`: The feeling of checking your ticket.
- `community-and-luck.html`: How shared luck impacts communities.

### 4. Guides & Info (7 Pages) - `/info/`
- `how-to-play.html`: Step-by-step guide for beginners.
- `tax-calculator-guide.html`: Understanding taxes on winnings.
- `faq.html`: Frequently Asked Questions.
- `about-us.html`: Our mission and team.
- `contact.html`: How to reach us.
- `privacy-policy.html`: Essential legal page for AdSense.
- `terms-of-service.html`: User agreement and disclaimer.
